Frequently Asked Questions


What is a Civil Aviation Idea Challenge?

Civil Aviation Idea Challenge by Triangulum Labls is a challenge for students to come up with solutions to real world problems pertaining to Civil Aviation. They can range from covering passenger journey facilitation from home to Airport (departing) and Airport to home (arriving passengers) besides in-airport experience at every touchpoint and more.


What are the rounds?

There are three rounds.
1. Round 1: Idea submission
2. Round 2: Idea pitching
3. Round 3: Working on the idea in an internship


Why Join?

Utilise your knowledge and your skills to solve real-world problems. This is also an opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals from the industry.


Who can apply?

The hackathon is open to students,employee,startups those who are interested in technology and innovation and have an interesting project or other idea to develop within the scope of the challenges proposed.Participation in the hackathon is open to individuals aged 18 or older.


Who judges the winners?

The winning team will be decided by a jury made up of industry experts and professionals of different corporations.


What are the Rewards?

Best idea will get a cash prize of 5 lakhs


Last date for registrations?

The registrations are open from 31-03-2020 to 30-05-2020


I am an intern/employee. Can I compete the Hackathon?

Yes, employees or other collaborators of any companies may compete in the hackathon


I just graduated, can I still compete?

Yes, If you have an idea to solve industry problem.

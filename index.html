Civil Aviation Idea Challenge

by Triangulum Labs in association with stuMagz


If you are a student who loves solving real world problems with your skills, then Civil Aviation Hackathon is THE challenge for you!

Civil Aviation Hackathon 2020

Elgibility:

There are over 40000 airports in the world which cater to billions of airline passengers. According to estimates by ICAO, 4.3 billion passengers flew in 2018 and the number is only going to rise more in the years to come. The Civil Aviation Hackathon is a challenge for students to come up with solutions to real world problems pertaining to Civil Aviation. They can range from covering passenger journey facilitation from home to Airport (departing) and Airport to home (arriving passengers) besides in-airport experience at every touchpoint and more.


If you are someone who wants to utilise your skills to solve these problems, then apply for this hackathon!


Students of any stream are eligible to participate in the program. A minimum team size of 1 to a maximum size of 4 students are allowed to be part of a team. You can take part individually or in a team of up to 4 students.


ROUND I

In the first round, students will submit their ideas and after screening, 50 teams/individual participants will be selected for the second round.



ROUND II

the top 50 teams/individual participants from the first round will have a session they will pitch their idea to an elite panel which includes experts from civil aviation industry and out of the 50, 10 teams/individual participants will then head to the third round.


ROUND III

The top 10 performers will get a paid internship from Triangulum Labs, and will work on their idea before presenting it to the final jury. Winners will receive a cash prize in addition to opportunities.
